Welcome to Agri-Market

Agri-Market offers farmers a platform to sell their products directly to consumers. By eliminating middlemen, farmers can retain more profit, and consumers can enjoy fresh, clean, and affordable produce.Agri-Market offers farmers a platform to sell their products directly to the consumers. Consumers can log in & see a variety of options.

They can search by product, or they can search by farmers. They can click on farmers & see their profiles where they'll see the farmers history & their whole list of products. Farmers can set their rates, update their stock, see their analytics, & manage everything through a dashboard. Users can purchase products straight on the app as well.

This way, the farmers skip the middlemen & keep more of the profit, while users pay less overall as they avoid the inflation tactics of middlemen & are ensured that their food is fresh & clean.
